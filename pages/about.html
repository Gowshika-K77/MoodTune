About MoodTune

A mood-based music player for English & K-Pop fans. No ads. No accounts. Just music.

🎭

8 Mood Categories

Happy, Sad, Energetic, Calm, Romantic, Angry, Focused, and Nostalgic — each with 25 hand-curated songs.

🌍

3 Languages

English hits and K-Pop bangers with curated playlists.

▶️

Spotify-Style Player

Full-featured player with progress bar, prev/next controls, volume, shuffle, and equalizer animation.

🚫

No Ads. No Login.

Pure music experience. No trackers, no pop-ups, no accounts required. Open and play instantly.

🔍

Smart Search

Search across all 200 songs by title, artist, or mood. Filter by language instantly.

🎨

Dark & Light Mode

Beautiful animated background with stars, aurora, and floating music notes. Supports dark and light themes.
